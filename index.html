Animation & Entertainment

Dive into our world through bite-sized, laugh-out-loud animation videos bursting with personality! Follow Kubo, the mischievous yet big-hearted protagonist, and their eclectic crew of friends as they embark on absurd adventures, tackle crypto-themed parodies, and drop relatable humor across TikTok, YouTube, and social media.

Whether you're here for the memes, the story, or the vibes, our content is designed to entertain, engage, and grow a tribe of loyal fans.

Community-Driven Shop

Create, Sell, Earn!

But we're not stopping at viral content. The Kubo & Friends Shop is where you take the spotlight. As a community member, you'll unlock tools to design and submit your own merchandise—think T-shirts, stickers, phone cases, and even wilder creations—all featuring Kubo & Friends characters.

Every product sold earns you a profit share, turning your creativity into crypto (or cash)! This isn't just a shop; it's a collaborative marketplace where fans become founders, and ideas become income.
